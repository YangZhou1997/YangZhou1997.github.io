Yang Zhou is a Postdoc at UC Berkeley SkyLab, working with Ion Stoica. He will join UC Davis as an Assistant Professor in Fall 2025. He got his Ph.D. from Harvard University, advised by Minlan Yu and James Mickens. He has equal interests in core systems and ML systems research, including efficient LLMs, GPU communication, and heterogeneous computing. As part of his research, he has actively collaborated with companies such as Google, Meta, and AWS. He is a recipient of a Google Ph.D. fellowship.
